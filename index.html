The Portraits

This work is an exploration of presence, grounded in the classical tradition of chiaroscuro. Each portrait is a collaboration, a quiet moment of observation intended to create a lasting and honest image, distinct from the fleeting nature of contemporary photography.

Comissions start at $1,000. Some work is paid for, but some work is called for -- if you are interested to sit as a subject, please reach out.
