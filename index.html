About

Good portraiture creates images that become more captivating with time. My great grandfather's portrait from a century ago possesses a timeless presence that inspired my approach to this work.

I create portraits that aren't meant for specific utility. The purpose is creating something people feel proud to display, and in which they see them best in themselves. These portraits are meant to be treasured by families and passed down through generations.

Portrait commissions start at $1500, including professional makeup and extended studio time. Sessions are unhurried allowing for careful lighting and multiple setups.

— Bill Van Besien
